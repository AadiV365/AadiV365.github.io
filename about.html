The CCP was established in 2018 by a group of enviromnentalis, scientists and concerned citizens. You can email us or call us for details on how to join your local chapter and contribute to your city.

Our Efforts

Urban Habitat Conservation: We conserve small parks, lakes and micro-habitats like public greenery. These areas can be adopted by donors, and the plants and animals within the habitat are taken care of and kept healthy by our volunteers.


Education and Training over many areas: We provide training on first aid for urban animals how to maintain an urban gardens, and effective composting.

The Conservation Project has been legally registered with the required Government authorities.
