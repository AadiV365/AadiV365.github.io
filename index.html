Make a difference in your city!

The CCP is a not-for-profit organization with the vision of conserving urban habitats and protecting the biodiversity of cities. We believe that just like us, all animals have a right to live in their habitats, and research has shown that being surrounded by nature is healthy for all of us.

Since our establishment in 2018 in the city of Bangalore, we have conserved thousands of city habitats for birds, street dogs and squirrels. We have cleaned up lakes to ensure safety of animals. We have protected habitats from being destroyed for profit. We request all of you to be part of our initiative, and help conserve the habitats around you.
